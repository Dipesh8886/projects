FOODONATION

Activity

Foodonation is a platform dedicated to helping those in need by redistributing surplus food from restaurants, dhabas, and eateries that would otherwise go to waste. With the support of volunteers, we make sure this leftover food reaches hungry people in the community. In return, the participating restaurants and dhabas benefit from increased visibility and positive reputation through social media. Every meal is carefully inspected by experts to ensure it’s safe to eat, so we can prevent any foodborne illnesses. By bridging the gap between surplus and need, Foodonation not only supports sustainability but also fosters a sense of community among volunteers, restaurants, and the people they serve. Together, we’re making a real difference—one meal at a time.
